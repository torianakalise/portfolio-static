Latest Projects

This semester was a course load that was difficult due to the Pandemic. This was a group project that I worked on with two other individuals, the objective of the project was to create a prototype of a funtional app using software like Axure RP9. This was a semester long project, where we continously made improvements based on a UX Design. We surveyed various groups of people and potential users for our application. The image used above was a still from the greeting screen from one of our intial prototypes.

Interview Video

This semester I had to complete a visual storytelling course, where we completed various audio and video projects. This is a still from my personal favorite project, It was a interview of my roomate, we had to ask the individual a series of questions and edit the video to tell a story without it seeming to be more of that standard interview.

Learning Theory

This course assignment pertains to my Human Resources and Development minor. We had to complete a five to six page paper on our own learning and how learning theory is incorperated in our courses we take. This was a challenging assignment because it asks you to take what you know about the theories and then see if any of your professors utilize them, and then not only see how and if they benefited your learning.

Photography

Photography is something I like to do in my free time, I like to explore the various things that an image can tell a viewer.
